Welcome To Jeopardy Online!

The Gameshow must go on!

Jeopardy is a quiz show that's about question and answer in which
contestants participate in and are asked questions and presented
with clues in the form of answers and contestants must answer in
the form of a question. Do you have what it takes?


How To Play
The computer will display a board and then from that board
you choose whichever question from whichever category you like.
The computer will display the question and the answers
and you have 60 seconds to answer. Good luck!
